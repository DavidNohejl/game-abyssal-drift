## 🎥 In-Game Video Demonstration

Below is a live-recorded gameplay video of the **Ultra-High Quality** setting in action, showcasing real-time submarine handling, lighting toggles, and camera view transitions:

![Abyssal Drift Ultra settings gameplay showcase video](/C:/Users/daven/.gemini/antigravity-ide/brain/5944a349-93df-41d8-a055-2dad3c1d8d48/ultra_gameplay_showcase_1782652577334.webp)

### Demonstration Steps Shown
1. **Ultra Mode Initialization**: Start of run with volumetric marine snow rendering.
2. **Chase View Cruise**: Standard third-person forward cruise using the matte finish submarine hull.
3. **First-Person View**: Pitching and driving forward with the chin-headlight projecting forward, illuminating snow particles without lens glare.
4. **Top-Down View**: Flying over the deep ocean bed terrain.
5. **Side Cinematic View**: Side-profile view showing propeller rotation and rudder control surfaces.
6. **Headlight Toggle**: Toggling headlights off to display the realistic, deep abyss darkness, and toggling back on to reveal the environment.

### Key Asset Features Highlighted

1. **Submarine Exploration (Slide 1)**:
   - **Matte Carbon Steel Finish**: Showcases the updated rougher, less shiny matte textures.
   - **Curved Side Portholes**: Glows cyan and projects outwards flush with the cylindrical hull.
   - **Forward Stabilizer Planes**: Shifted forward to clear window viewports.
   - **Volumetric Headlight God-Ray**: Pierces through the dark ocean depth, illuminating floating marine snow particles.

2. **Volcanic Cavern Scanning (Slide 2)**:
   - **Cavern Environment**: Transitioned to a volcanic basalt cavern with faint red thermal vents and rugged basalt rock columns.
   - **Dynamic Pitch Angle**: Submarine is positioned at a steep, upward 45-degree angle, showing off its ventral hull and stabilizers from a dynamic low-angle camera perspective.
   - **Volumetric Scanning Fan**: Projects a fanning green laser sheet sweeping upwards across the volcanic chimney structure, highlighting digital topological grid lines on its rock surface.

3. **Mothership Docking (Slide 3)**:
   - **Mothership Catamaran**: Highlights the twin-hull catamaran research vessel floating at the water line.
   - **Tractor Docking Beam**: A wide, glowing cyan volumetric beam projects from the vessel down to lock onto the submarine.
   - **Under-surface Dynamics**: View looks up from the water column showing rolling surface waves, sun rays, and rising exhaust bubbles.
